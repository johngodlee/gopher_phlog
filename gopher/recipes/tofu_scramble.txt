Tofu scramble

Ingredients

-   1 block firm tofu
-   2 spring onions
-   0.5 tsp turmeric
-   2 tbsp nutritional yeast flakes
-   0.75 tsp paprika
-   0.75 tsp chives
-   1.75 tsp garlic powder
-   Salt
-   Pepper

Method

1.  Slice spring onions, saute in a large skillet over medium heat
2.  Crumble tofu into pan, breaking up larger pieces
3.  Add turmeric, nutrional yeast, paprika, chives, garlic powder,
    salt, pepper
4.  Fry until tofu is golden brown
